## Services We Offer

MIRA Forward New England provides a range of services to support immigrant communities.

### Legal Assistance

Access to immigration legal services and referrals.

### Community Support

Resources and programs designed to help immigrants integrate and thrive.

### Education Programs

English language classes and educational resources for all ages.

### Advocacy

Working to advance policies that support immigrant rights and well-being.
