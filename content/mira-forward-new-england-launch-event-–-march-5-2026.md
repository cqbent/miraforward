***Speaking Program begins at 12:15 p.m.*** 

At MIRA Forward New England, we envision a nation where immigrants and refugees can fully thrive, contribute to the social, economic, and civic life of our communities, and see themselves represented at all levels of government. Together, we mobilize, support, and campaign for candidates and policies that reflect the values and priorities of immigrant communities.
