MIRA Forward builds the political power needed to advance pro-immigrant policies and defend immigrant communities. Through advocacy, organizing, and direct engagement with policymakers, MIRA Forward works to ensure immigrant voices are heard and that our communities have a seat at the table when decisions are made.

Your support helps strengthen our ability to push for lasting policy change and hold elected leaders accountable.

## **Ways to Give**

MIRA Forward currently accepts donations by **check, money order, and ACH transfer**.

To make a gift by **ACH**, please contact **Daisy Guzmán, Director of Development** at [development@miraforward.org](mailto:development@miraforward.org) for transfer instructions.

Checks and money orders can be made payable to **MIRA Forward New England** and mailed to:

Boston, MA 02114

Attn: Development Department
