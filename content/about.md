## About MIRA Forward New England

**MIRA Forward New England** is dedicated to supporting immigrant communities across the New England region.

### Our Mission

We work to empower immigrants and refugees through advocacy, education, and community support services.

### Our Vision

A New England where all immigrants have the opportunity to thrive and contribute to their communities.
